Welcome to AI interactive teacher

The ionic app is a practical preview of the machine learning technologies in action, and a demonstration of proper demos in use.

Why is AI interacitve techer?

The purpose of this AI interactive teacher is to assist teachers as they prepare students to take AI courses in the online system. The Interactive Demo(Demo) is a practice tool designed to allow students to practice before taking a course. This app was designed to help teachers guide students through the Demo, ensuring that students practice all the tools and functionality available.

What is AI technology?

In computer science, artificial intelligence (AI), sometimes called machine intelligence, is intelligence demonstrated by machines, in contrast to the natural intelligence displayed by humans.
